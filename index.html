Today nearly 1 billion people don't have access to clean and safe water. WHOLE WORLD Water is a campaign designed to unite the hospitality and tourism industries on a non-competitive platform to eradicate this issue. With the help of their global sponsors and growing member base around the world, wholeworldwater.co is working towards raising $1 billion dollars annually. The concept is simple - Businesses filter and sell their own water and contribute 10% of the proceeds to the Whole World Water Fund. If you work in the hospitality and tourism industry, check out Wholeworldwater.co and see how you can make a whole world of difference.
